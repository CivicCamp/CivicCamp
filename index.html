Civic Camp

Sun Jul 10, 2016 2016

@ United Nations HQ

A conference focused on the creation, use and sharing of innovative open source solutions for civic, public sector and government needs.

We are a community-organized conference, run on a not-for-profit basis by open source community volunteers.

A key objective of Civic Camp is to help support creation of a turnkey and comprehensive suite of free open source software solutions for major governmental needs.

We do this through a combination of presentation, collaboration, fundraising and code/contribution sprints.
